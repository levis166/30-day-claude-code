# Fashion Editorial Style Shoot, But For Furniture. Think Chanel, But B&B Italia In The 80/90'S. Vogue Living, Bodies On Furntiture, Grain, Slower Shutter, Movement. Elegant But Gritty. Modern But Classic Mood Board

---

## Creative Direction

Capture furniture as haute couture. Bodies move through and interact with pieces as sculptural elements. Embrace film grain, slower shutter speeds, and natural light to create movement and tactility. The aesthetic bridges Italian modernist design with Parisian fashion editorials—timeless silhouettes, neutral palette, with emphasis on texture, material, and the human form in relation to space. Gritty refinement: elegant but undone.

---

## Typography

- **Heading:** Bodoni MT or Didot
- **Body:** Garamond or Caslon
- **Rationale:** High-fashion serif fonts evoke 1980s-90s editorial luxury while maintaining modern sophistication. Elegant but with slight vintage grain quality.
